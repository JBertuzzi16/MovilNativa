SPIN 36

Game guide

How to play

Each session starts with 50 coins. Choose a bet type, enter the amount you want to wager and press SPIN. The roulette will pick a number from 0 to 36 and calculate your prize automatically.

Bet types

Straight x36 Exact number (0–36)
Dozen x3 1st (1–12) · 2nd (13–24) · 3rd (25–36)
Red / Black x2 0 is neither red nor black
Even / Odd x2 0 does not count as even or odd

Streak system

Each consecutive win adds 1 streak point. Reaching 5 wins in a row awards a +100 coin bonus and resets the streak. Any loss resets it to 0.

Sessions and history

A new session opens every time you start playing. When you leave, the session closes and is saved in the history with the following data:

  • Start and end date/time
  • Final balance obtained
  • Maximum streak achieved
  • Total number of bets placed

Saving victories

When you win a bet, the app offers to save a victory image to your device. You can choose the destination folder and file name. The image is saved in high-quality WebP format.

Settings

  • Enable or disable background music.
  • Adjust the music volume.
  • Choose a custom song from your device.
